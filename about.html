About Us

What we do Who are we Why we do it

Connecting Best Friends

We provide you with an easy-to-use application for your mobile device in which you can find a doggo friend for your doggo within a few minutes!

We want to give you the freedom and enough information of all possible dog friends around your area so you can set up playdates for your pet and new BFF!

The DREAM Team

8 strangers RANDOMLY became a group during a class led by Dr. Leinecker at the University of Central Florida. Without that much web or app experience, the DREAM team had to work harder than other teams in order to make this application come to life.

And here we are TODAY.

We LOVE Dogs

This is easy, we LOVE dogs. We enjoy being with them and we believe they should be able to have more friends than just us, humans!

Connecting dogs and dog owners is our main goal. That is why this application came to life. And we hope you love it too.
