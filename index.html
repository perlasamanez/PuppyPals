Create a Profile for Your Best Friend

Creating a profile for your furry friend is easy! Just go to our app and follow the simple steps. Make sure to add as much information about your doggo so you get the most matches!

Set. Camera. Action!

Look for that Best Friend

Once you are done with your profile, you will be able to swipe left or right to find that best friend for your doggo! Our algorithms will allow you to see matches based on the preferences you set with your profile!

Now that you have your matches, it's time to get in touch! Contact the other dog's owners to set up a playdate. Make sure it's always in a public place like a park. For sure your furry friend and BFF would want to RUN!

FAQ

What is your question?

What is PuppyPals anyways?

PuppyPals is a web/app platform that allows you to find doggo friends for YOUR doggo. Our algorithms will allow you to find matches based on your preferences.

Is this app like Tinder?

While our design is very similar to Tinder, we are looking to promote a different purpose. To find a playmate for your dog.

Is this app free?

YES. PuppyPals is 100% free. We will also provide premium memberships in the future, so be sure to check it out!

Are cats allowed in the app?

We love ALL pets and do NOT discriminate, but this app has been made EXCLUSIVELY for dogs, so no! (Perhaps, we may make one for cats. Who knows?)

Can I delete my account when I want to?

PuppyPals believes in freedom. So yes, feel free to leave whenever you want. We would miss you though!

Can't find my question here!

That's all right! Connect with us and we will make sure to answer any questions you may have!
